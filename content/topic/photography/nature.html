Landscapes

Afternoon by the Darßwald

Captured on March 6, 2024, while walking along the western shore of the Fischland-Darß-Zingst peninsula towards the Darßer Ort headland in the north. This part of the Vorpommersche Boddenlandschaft national park is known for its steep sandy coasts. I hope these pictures convey the at times harsh conditions that shape such a unique environment.

Weekend in the Schwarzwald

Dawn at the Himmelstreppe

Captured during sunrise on September 22, 2024, atop the Halde Rheinelbe mine dump in Gelsenkirchen-Ückendorf next to the Himmelstreppe sculpture. Depicted are views of the surrounding parts of the Ruhrgebiet region. I especially like the soft colors as well as the low angle of the morning light.
