Landscapes

Afternoon in the Darßwald

Captured on March 6, 2024, while walking along the western shore of the Darß peninsula towards the Darßer Ort headland in the north. This part of the Vorpommersche Boddenlandschaft national park is known for its steep sandy coast. I hope the pictures convey the at times harsh conditions that shape such a unique environment.

Dawn at the Himmelstreppe

Captured during sunrise on September 22, 2024, atop the Halde Rheinelbe mine dump in Gelsenkirchen-Ückendorf next to the Himmelstreppe sculpture. Depicted are views of the surrounding parts of the Ruhrgebiet region. I especially like the soft colors as well as the low angle of the morning light.
